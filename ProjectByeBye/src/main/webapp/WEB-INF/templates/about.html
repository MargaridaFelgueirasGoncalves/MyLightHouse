OUR MISSION:

Lighthouse is born from a necessity to help those lost in the sea of doubt, darkness, and violence.

Too many people suffer in silence from domestic violence and live in fear of getting out and even the slightest will to change, is hard, many times shameful and involves too many complex steps.

Our job is simple, you need one step, the one towards us, towards the lightHouse, the rest is up to us!
